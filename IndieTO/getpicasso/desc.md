## Picasso > Picasso is a powerful native tool to create professional App Store screenshots with ease.

## Basic Information - Website: https://getpicasso.com/ - Pricing: Free trial, paid subscription ($12/month or $99/year) - Platforms: macOS, iOS

## Tool Introduction - Picasso is an intuitive screenshot editing app that streamlines the process of creating stunning App Store visuals. With its user-friendly interface and robust features, Picasso empowers developers to craft professional-grade screenshots effortlessly, saving valuable time and effort.

## Key Features - Automatically adds device frames to simulator screenshots - Allows adding images, text, and annotations to screenshots - Spans elements seamlessly across multiple screenshots - Supports localization with .xcstrings file integration - Offers 40+ device frames for all Apple platforms

## Suitable Use Cases - App developers creating screenshots for App Store product pages - Creators needing visuals with device frames for marketing assets - UI/UX designers showcasing app designs with realistic device mockups

## Usage Tips - Organize screenshots into project folders for better management - Utilize the span tool to duplicate elements across screenshots - Export screenshots directly to App Store Connect for easy uploads
