## AppShot Gallery > The ultimate gallery for discovering and filtering app screenshots by style

## Basic Information - Website: https://www.appshot.gallery/ - Pricing: Free - Platform: Web

## Tool Introduction - AppShot Gallery is a visually appealing website that curates and showcases screenshots from various mobile apps, allowing users to explore and find inspiration for their app designs and marketing strategies.

## Key Features - Extensive filtering options to search by genre, tone, color, font style, and visual styles like 3D, pixel art, hand-drawn, etc.
- Ability to submit your own app for inclusion in the gallery - Clean and modern design for an enjoyable browsing experience

## Use Cases - App developers looking for design inspiration or references for their apps - Marketers seeking attractive screenshots to use in app store listings - Designers studying app UI/UX trends and styles

## Tips - Take advantage of the detailed filters to narrow down to your preferred aesthetics - Check out the "New" section regularly for the latest additions - Look for well-rated apps in your niche for ideas on visuals that resonate
