## Screenshots Pro > A powerful, online screenshot generator to create beautiful app previews for the App Store and Google Play.

## Basic Information - Official Website: https://screenshots.pro/ - Pricing: Free plan + paid subscription plans (Standard at $19/month, Extended at $49/month) - Platforms: Web

## Tool Introduction
Screenshots Pro is a game-changing online tool that simplifies the process of creating stunning screenshot mockups for your iOS and Android apps. With its user-friendly editor, you can effortlessly frame your app screenshots into the latest device models, apply various effects, and localize content for different regions - all within a matter of minutes.

## Key Features - Massive library of device frames (23+ Apple and Android models) - Supports portrait and landscape orientations - 3D perspective mockups for a premium look - Localization to translate screenshots into multiple languages - Auto-resizing for required App Store and Play Store sizes

## Ideal Use Cases - Developers and designers creating app listing pages - Startups preparing app marketing materials - Agencies designing assets for client app launches - Individual app creators polishing their product visuals

## Getting Started
1. Browse the pre-made templates or start from scratch
2. Upload your app screenshots and frame them on desired devices
3. Customize colors, angles, languages, etc. using the intuitive editor
4. Download high-res exports optimized for app stores
