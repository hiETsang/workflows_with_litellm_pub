## Uneed

> Uneed is a democratic launch platform for discovering and promoting tech products and tools.

## Basic Information
- Website: https://www.uneed.best/
- Pricing: Freemium (paid plans for promoting tools)
- Platform: Web

## Tool Introduction
- Uneed is a vibrant community where makers can showcase their latest creations to get visibility, feedback and support from a diverse audience. Users can explore fresh tech tools daily, across various categories like development, design, marketing and more. Uneed aims to provide a fair stage for all innovations to shine.

## Key Features
- Daily launches of new products across multiple categories
- Upvoting system to surface trending tools 
- Platform for makers to promote their tools
- Community leaderboard for active contributors
- Directories of top tools in specific niches

## Use Cases
- Discover innovative tools and software across different domains
- Seek inspiration for new product ideas from trending launches
- Get early user feedback and traction for your product launches
- Connect with an engaged community of creators and early adopters

## Pricing Plans
- Free: Basic access to browse products
- Maker Pro: $25/month, promote unlimited tools, additional stats
- Business: $100/month, enhanced promotion, priority support  

## Pros & Cons
- Pros: Fresh product discovery, maker-friendly, active community
- Cons: No Android/iOS apps, limited advanced filtering options

So in summary, Uneed is a great place for makers to launch new tools and for early adopters to find innovative apps and software across different categories. The voting system and community features make it a lively hub for product makers.
