## Shots
> Create stunning mockups for social media, websites, and more with ease.

## Basic Info
- Website: https://shots.so/
- Pricing: Free (beta)
- Platform: Web

## Tool Introduction
- Shots is a user-friendly web app that simplifies the process of creating pixel-perfect mockups for digital products. With its intuitive drag-and-drop interface and extensive customization options, it saves designers valuable time and effort.

## Key Features
- Wide variety of professionally-designed mockup templates for different use cases
- Ability to customize backgrounds, layouts, and frames with just a few clicks
- Export high-resolution images optimized for various social media platforms

## Ideal Use Cases
- Product designers creating mockups for websites, mobile apps, and web apps
- Digital marketers crafting visuals for social media campaigns
- Freelancers and agencies delivering high-quality mockups to clients

## Getting Started
1. Visit https://shots.so/ and upload or drag-and-drop your design file
2. Choose a suitable mockup template from the available options
3. Customize the mockup using the editing tools provided
4. Export the final mockup as a high-res image file

## Usage Tips 
- Prepare your design files in PNG or JPEG format for best results
- Explore the pre-made layout options to quickly revamp your mockup style
- Utilize the hand-picked background packs for added visual appeal
