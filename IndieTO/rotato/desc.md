## Rotato > An easy-to-use 3D mockup generator and animator for showcasing mobile app/web designs

## Basic Information - Website: https://rotato.app/ - Pricing: Free with paid Pro plans (subscription) - Platforms: Mac

## Tool Description - Rotato is a specialized app that allows designers to quickly create high-quality 3D mockups of their app or website designs on various device models like iPhones, Android phones, laptops etc. It offers a simple drag-and-drop interface to import designs and animate them in a realistic 3D environment.

## Key Features - Massive library of over 30 pre-made, photorealistic 3D device models - Ability to fully customize device colors, materials, backgrounds etc.
- Advanced camera controls to adjust perspective, depth of field etc.
- Animation timeline for creating app preview videos - Figma plugin for direct import of designs

## Use Cases - Creating eye-catching 3D mockup images/videos to showcase app/website UIs - Building impressive product demos, portfolio pieces, pitch decks - Generating high-quality assets for App Store listings or marketing materials
